Wallet AI - Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 5, 2026

1. Introduction

Wallet AI ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal finance management application that helps you track expenses, manage budgets, and achieve your financial goals with AI-powered insights and gamification features. Your privacy is important to us.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address when you create an account.
  • Financial Data: Expense amounts, categories, budget limits, savings goals, and subscription details that you manually enter.
  • Preferences: Currency, language, spending style, and notification preferences.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: App usage patterns such as login streaks, XP earned, and badges achieved (gamification data).
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system version for compatibility purposes.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT access your bank accounts or financial institutions.
  • We do NOT collect location data.
  • We do NOT use advertising trackers.
  • We do NOT sell your data to third parties.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide expense tracking and budget management features.
  • To generate AI-powered financial analysis and personalized recommendations.
  • To manage your gamification progress (XP, levels, badges, streaks).
  • To sync your data across devices (if you opt in).
  • To send you optional notifications (daily reminders, weekly summaries).

4. AI Analysis

Our AI features analyze your spending patterns to provide financial insights. This analysis:

  • Uses only the financial data you manually enter in the app.
  • Is processed through secure, encrypted API connections.
  • Does not store your data on AI provider servers beyond the request processing time.
  • Can be disabled at any time in your settings.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your financial data is primarily stored locally on your device using encrypted SQLite database.
  • Secure Storage: Sensitive information (API keys, authentication tokens) is stored in iOS Keychain / Android Keystore.
  • Cloud Sync: If enabled, data is synced via Supabase with PostgreSQL, using industry-standard encryption (TLS 1.2+).
  • Biometric Protection: The app supports Face ID / Touch ID for additional security.

6. Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data may only be shared in the following circumstances:

  • When required by law or legal process.
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property.

7. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app.
  • Export: Premium users can export their data as CSV files.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile screen.
  • Opt-out: You can disable notifications, AI analysis, and cloud sync independently.

8. Children's Privacy

Wallet AI is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. In-App Purchases

Wallet AI offers optional premium subscriptions ($6.99/month or $49.99/year). Purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app and updating the "Last updated" date.

11. No Advertisements

Wallet AI does not display any advertisements. We do not use any advertising SDKs or tracking frameworks.
