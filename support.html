Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track an expense?

Tap the "+" button on the dashboard to add a new expense. Enter the amount, select a category, and optionally add a note.

How does the AI analysis work?

Wallet AI analyzes your spending patterns to provide personalized financial insights. The AI generates weekly and monthly reports with actionable recommendations.

Is my financial data secure?

Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device. Sensitive information is protected using iOS Keychain encryption. We never access your bank accounts.

What is the XP system?

You earn Experience Points (XP) for using the app: logging expenses (+5 XP), daily logins (+10 XP), staying within budget (+15 XP), and completing challenges (+50-200 XP). XP helps you level up from "Money Apprentice" to "Legendary Financier"!

What does Premium include?

Premium ($6.99/month or $49.99/year) unlocks: unlimited AI analysis, unlimited savings goals, unlimited custom categories, receipt scanning, CSV export, and advanced charts.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iPhone, find Wallet AI, and tap "Cancel Subscription".

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile > Settings > Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data.
